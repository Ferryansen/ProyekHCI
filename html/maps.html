Rain Forest

The rain forest is one of the jungles of Brazil. In the forest, there are various kinds of monsters inside. When someone enters this forest, that person will not be able to get out of the forest. So far, no one has been able to get out of the forest.

War Zone

War zone formerly named as the city of knowledge. But, when the empire's army clashed with the Enigma, the city of glory turned into a deadly place to live. Nevertheless, the Enigma strives to take back War zone and turn it back as it's glorious days.

Trap Temple

Trap temple is one of Egypt temple which has been forsaken for hundreds of year. However, since a certain historian discover there’s a huge treasure sleeping somewhere inside the temple, hundreds of agents have gone there and they must fight each other to achieve the treasure. Inside the temple, they must not only fight the other agent, but they must also survive from the secret trap hidden in the temple. Not only that, there’s also mummy and other moving dolls which will attack the agent to protect the treasure.
